## Reporting a Vulnerability

We take the security of our software seriously. If you believe you have found a security vulnerability in Push to Hold, please report it to us as described below.

**Please do not report security vulnerabilities through public GitHub issues.**

### How to Report

Please send an email to [INSERT EMAIL ADDRESS] with the subject "Security Vulnerability Report".

In your report, please include:

- A description of the vulnerability.
- Steps to reproduce the issue.
- Any relevant logs or screenshots.
- Your contact information so we can follow up with you.

### Response Timeline

We will acknowledge receipt of your report within 48 hours. We will then investigate the issue and keep you updated on our progress. We aim to resolve all security vulnerabilities as quickly as possible.

### Disclosure Policy

We ask that you give us a reasonable amount of time to fix the vulnerability before disclosing it to the public. We will coordinate with you on the public disclosure timeline.

Thank you for helping keep Push to Hold secure!
